About

Code Sangam 2026 is a student-led revolution in learning — an open-source development arena created by YantraYodha, the hardware and coding club of ECE Department (Abacus Institute of Engineering and Management). This isn’t another hackathon or workshop that ends in a weekend; it’s a living ecosystem where students join real open-source projects and grow through genuine industry-style collaboration. Starting from 20 January 2026 and continuing as long as our projects evolve, Code Sangam turns learners into contributors, coders into creators, and classrooms into communities. Every session, every commit, and every discussion moves students one step closer to becoming job-ready professionals who understand how real software and hardware are built.

Why this Event

Code Sangam 2026 exists to challenge the corporatized learning system that profits from students while delivering little real-world skill. We believe skill should never have a price tag — it should be shared, practiced, and proven through contribution. This initiative gives every participant a chance to experience true open-source collaboration, industry workflows, and modern development tools — without paying a single rupee. By contributing to live community projects, students gain professional-level exposure, teamwork experience, and a public portfolio that can open internship and career doors faster than any paid course ever could. This is not just an event; it’s a movement to make knowledge open, equal, and effective.

Who Can Take Part

Code Sangam 2026 welcomes every YantraYodha member who believes learning happens by doing. Whether you’re a beginner curious about your first pull request or a developer eager to architect systems, there’s space for you here. Participants go through a creative-and-logic-based test that helps match them to suitable project domains — from frontend and API development to full-stack and Python-based software projects. Once a student commits to a project, they become part of a living team, mentored by former organizers and club alumni who guide them through real challenges. If you’re ready to learn, to code with purpose, and to stand for open, community-driven education, Code Sangam 2026 is your battlefield and your opportunity.
